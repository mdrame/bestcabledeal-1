About Us

Here at ALTERNATIVE VISUAL MEDIA (AVM), we provide unbeatable streaming services. You’ll be able to enjoy quality entertainment whenever you want, wherever you go.

Our team grew tired of the constant frustrations that we were experiencing with traditional TV. It seems like we would always miss our favorite shows, scroll endlessly looking for a decent show, or have the wrong TV package without access to the channels that we wanted to watch. After our time struggling with traditional TV and other unhelpful streaming platforms, we decided that enough was enough!

AVM eliminates many problems of traditional TV and offers innovative solutions to increase your viewing pleasure. Best of all, AVM is backed by our free customer support and free unlimited updates to ensure you get the quality TV you deserve.

We pride ourselves on delivering the best services and solutions. You won't regret joining the AVM family of customers, you'll just wonder what took you so long.
